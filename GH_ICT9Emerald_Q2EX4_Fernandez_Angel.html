Space vs. Universe

Space

The distance between celestial bodies, such as the planet Earth, is also referred to as outer space. It is made up of a hard vacuum with a low density of particles, primarily a plasma of helium and hydrogen. Additionally, it consists of neutrinos, electromagnetic radiation, magnetic fields, dust, and cosmic rays.






Universe

Everything that exists can be considered to be a part of the universe. It is made up of all known forms of physical matter and energy, as well as solar systems, planets, galaxies, and all other space-related objects.
